Are There Changes In Eating Habits That Suggest Illness?

Yes, changes in eating habits can indicate that your fish might be sick.

A healthy fish shows a strong interest in food and comes to eat when it’s feeding time. If your fish usually eats aggressively but suddenly loses its appetite, this could be a sign of stress or illness.

Sometimes, a sick fish might start to eat less over time or completely stop eating. Observe if it’s ignoring food that it used to like. Another clue is how the fish handles food. Fish that are unwell may take food into their mouth and then spit it out repeatedly. This isn’t normal and suggests there’s an issue. Keep an eye on these behaviors during feeding times to catch any potential problems early. If you notice these habits, consider consulting with a vet who specializes in fish.
