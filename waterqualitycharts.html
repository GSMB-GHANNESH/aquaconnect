Water Quality Components for Healthy Fish

Component Optimal Range Importance
Temperature Warmwater: 25–30°C, Coldwater: 10–18°C Affects metabolism, growth, and immunity
Dissolved Oxygen 5–10 mg/L Essential for respiration
pH 6.5–8.5 Maintains physiological balance
Ammonia (NH₃) < 0.02 mg/L Prevents toxicity and gill damage
Nitrites (NO₂⁻) < 0.1 mg/L Prevents "brown blood disease"
Nitrates (NO₃⁻) < 50 mg/L Limits stress and promotes immunity
Salinity Freshwater: <0.5 ppt, Marine: 30–35 ppt Maintains osmotic balance
Turbidity < 5 NTU Affects water clarity and gill health
Hardness 50–150 mg/L CaCO₃ Supports osmoregulation and buffering
Alkalinity 50–200 mg/L CaCO₃ Stabilizes pH levels
Carbon Dioxide < 10 mg/L Prevents oxygen displacement
Chlorine 0 mg/L Avoids toxicity and gill damage
